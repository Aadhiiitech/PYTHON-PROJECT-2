
Introduction: 
The Titanic dataset is used to study passenger survival based on factors like age, gender, and class. This analysis helps understand which factors influenced survival chances.

Dataset Overview: 
The dataset contains passenger details such as age, sex, class, and survival status. It includes both numerical and categorical variables with some missing values.

Methodology & Statistical Analysis: 
Data was cleaned and grouped by gender, class, and age to calculate survival rates. Simple statistical methods like percentages and comparisons were used to identify patterns.

Conclusion: 
Survival was higher for females and first-class passengers compared to others. The results show that social and demographic factors played an important role in survival.
